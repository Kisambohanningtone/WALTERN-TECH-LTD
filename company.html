About Waltern Tech

Waltern Tech Ltd is a forward-thinking technology and fintech solutions company dedicated to transforming how businesses operate in the digital age. We design and develop enterprise-grade websites, smart point-of-sale (POS) systems, payment integration platforms, and custom business software tailored to modern business needs. At Waltern Tech Ltd, we believe technology should be powerful, scalable, and secure. Our team focuses on building reliable digital infrastructure that supports financial transactions, streamlines business processes, and connects businesses to their customers more efficiently. We are committed to innovation, performance, and delivering technology solutions that help businesses grow, automate operations, and succeed in an increasingly digital world.

2025 – Year Founded

As a payment infrastructure, Waltern Tech helps African businesses go global while enabling multinational corporations to scale in Africa.
